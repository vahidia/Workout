4-Day Workout Plan

Day 1: Chest

  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Arm Circles: 2 sets x 30 seconds each direction

Day 2: Back

  • T-Pushups: 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Bent-over Dumbbell Rows with a Twist: 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 3: Shoulders

  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raise: 3 sets x 12 reps

Day 4: Core

  • Plank: 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets x 15 reps each side
